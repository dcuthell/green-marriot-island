Resort Activites

Green Marriot Island has plenty of activites to enjoy for people of all ages and interests

  • Shark Fishing
    • The resort regularly discards food waste off shore to encourage a thriving fish habitat that has recently been a hotspot for sharks. Head out with our experienced Captain Quint for your shark fishing adventure!
  • Jungle Tours
    • We regularly lose guests who choose our sleeping bag accomodation option deep within the jungle. Maybe you can find them?
  • Aqua Aerobics
    • Enjoy the coolness of the pool while getting a full body workout. Our aqua aerobics program includes exercises like aqua squats, aqua deadlifts, aqua kettlebell swings, and aqua situps.

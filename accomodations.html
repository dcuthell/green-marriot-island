Accomodation Options

Green Marriot Island offers a variety of lodging choices, listed below

Palace

  • The Palace offers 10000sq ft of space, 20 bedrooms with king sized beds, 10 hot tubs, an olympic sized pool, and a trained elephant butler

Bungalow

  • Our quaint bungalows can be found around the pools of the resorts, as well as near the beach. Each bungalow comfortably sleeps 4, and has high speed wifi available for an additonal fee

Sleeping Bag

  • Our most inexpensive lodging option is perfect for younger travellers and those with a sense of adventure. We will provide a thin sleeping bag, a gallon of water, and point you in the direction of the less dangerous parts of the jungle.
